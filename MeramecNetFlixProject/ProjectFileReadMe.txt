Step 1: You must add a reference to your custom MerameNetFlixLibrary dll to your MeramecNetFlixProject in order to use the
dll in your Data Access Layer routines

Step 2: Right-click the References under the MeramecNetFlixProject and select Add References.

Step 3: Browse to your MeramecNetFlixComponentProject on your local system to located the dll
called MeramecNetFlixComponentLibrary.dll. This might be either the \bin\debug or \bin\release folder. 
Select the dll and it will be added as a new reference to your solution.

Step 4: Will demostrate how to use the library calls in class.
